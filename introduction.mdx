FitStart Planner is a free, beginner-friendly fitness planning app that runs entirely in your browser. Whether you're taking your first steps toward a healthier lifestyle or looking for a simple, no-fuss way to organize your workouts and meals, FitStart Planner gives you everything you need in one place — no account, no installation, and no personal data sent anywhere. Open the app, start exploring, and build a routine that actually fits your life.

## What You Can Do with FitStart Planner

FitStart Planner is organized into four focused sections, each designed to support a different part of your fitness journey. Together, they give you a complete picture — from choosing the right workout to fueling your body well.

## No Account Required

FitStart Planner is designed to get out of your way. There's no signup form, no password to remember, and no app to download. Open it in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — and start using it immediately. Your weekly plan is saved directly in your browser's local storage, which means your data stays on your device and is never shared with a server.

<Info>
  Because your weekly plan is stored in your browser's localStorage, it persists between visits on the same device and browser. Clearing your browser data or switching devices will reset your plan.
</Info>

## Who FitStart Planner Is For

FitStart Planner is built specifically for people who are new to structured fitness. If you've ever felt overwhelmed by complicated training apps, unsure which workout to try first, or confused about how to eat in support of your goals — this app is for you. Every workout in the library is labeled by goal and difficulty level, all guidance is jargon-free, and the weekly planner keeps your routine visible and manageable.

You don't need any prior fitness experience to get started. FitStart Planner meets you exactly where you are.
