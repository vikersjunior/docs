Getting started with FitStart Planner takes less than five minutes. Because there's nothing to install or sign up for, your only job is to open the app and start exploring. This guide walks you through the four main pages and the recommended order to visit them so you leave with a workout picked, a weekly plan started, and a clear sense of how to keep the momentum going.

<Note>
  FitStart Planner works on both mobile and desktop browsers. The layout adapts to your screen size, so you can plan your week on a laptop and check your routine on your phone without losing anything.
</Note>

## Your First Five Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open FitStart Planner in Your Browser">
    Navigate to the FitStart Planner URL in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge all work. No download, no login, and no account creation required. The app loads instantly and is ready to use straight away.

    You'll land on the **Home page**, which gives you a snapshot of the app's tools: a BMI calculator, a random workout generator, a daily fitness tip, and previews of available workout routines. Take a moment to scroll through — it's a great orientation to what the app offers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Workout Library">
    Click **Workouts** in the navigation to open the full workout library. This is the best place to start if you're not sure what to do first.

    The library contains six beginner-friendly routines:

    | Routine | Duration | Focus |
    |---|---|---|
    | Morning Stretch Routine | 10 min | Mobility |
    | Beginner Cardio Blast | 20 min | Endurance |
    | Core Strength Builder | 15 min | Strength |
    | Fat Burn Circuit | 25 min | Weight Loss |
    | Walking Recovery Flow | 18 min | Mobility |
    | Starter Strength Session | 22 min | Strength |

    Use the filters to narrow your options by **goal** (Weight Loss, Strength, Mobility, Endurance) or **level** (Beginner, Easy, Intermediate), or type a keyword into the search bar. Pick one routine that appeals to you — you'll add it to your weekly plan in the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your First Weekly Plan">
    Click **Weekly Plan** in the navigation to open the planner. Here you'll see a view of the full week — Monday through Sunday — with each day ready to accept workout sessions.

    Add the routine you chose in the previous step to a day that works for your schedule. You can add multiple sessions per day and remove any session you change your mind about. Work through the week and assign workouts to the days you plan to exercise, leaving rest days empty.

    <Tip>
      Your weekly plan saves automatically to your browser. You don't need to click a save button — just close the tab and your plan will be waiting when you return on the same device and browser.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Nutrition Page">
    Click **Nutrition** in the navigation to explore eating guidance that supports your new routine. The page covers three areas:

    - **Balanced Plate Guide** — a visual breakdown of how to proportion protein, carbohydrates, and vegetables in a meal
    - **Water Intake Calculator** — enter your weight and activity level to get a personalized daily hydration target
    - **Random Meal Idea Generator** — tap the button to surface a balanced meal suggestion when you need quick inspiration

    You don't need to follow a strict diet plan. Use the Nutrition page as a reference to make small, sustainable improvements to how you eat alongside your new workout schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Homepage Tools Daily">
    Return to the **Home page** to make use of the quick-access tools whenever you need a boost:

    - **BMI Calculator** — enter your height and weight to get your Body Mass Index as a general health reference point
    - **Random Workout Generator** — tap the button for a surprise workout suggestion on days when you're not sure what to do
    - **Daily Tip Generator** — generate a fresh fitness or wellness tip to keep you informed and motivated

    Making a habit of checking the homepage each morning is a simple way to stay engaged with your goals without any extra effort.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Do Next

Once you've completed your first week, come back to the Weekly Planner and refresh your schedule. Swap in new routines from the Workout Library to keep things varied, and revisit the Nutrition page as your habits evolve.

<Warning>
  Your weekly plan is tied to your specific browser and device. If you clear your browser's site data or use a different browser, your saved plan will not carry over. Consider taking a quick screenshot of your plan if you want a portable record of it.
</Warning>
