FitStart Planner is built to be as frictionless as possible, so you can spend less time figuring out the app and more time moving. Whether you're wondering how your data is stored, what equipment you'll need, or how to get the most out of the workout library, the answers below cover everything you're likely to run into as you get started.

<Accordion title="Do I need an account or login to use FitStart Planner?">
  No account or login is required — ever. FitStart Planner runs entirely in your browser, so you can open the app and start planning your first week of workouts instantly. There's nothing to install, no email to verify, and no password to remember.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my data saved anywhere?">
  Yes. Your weekly plan is automatically saved to your browser's **localStorage** under the key `fitstart_weekly_plan`. This means your sessions are stored locally on your device rather than on a remote server.

  <Warning>
    Clearing your browser's cache, cookies, or site data will permanently erase your saved weekly plan. If you want to preserve your schedule, take a screenshot or note it down before clearing your browser data.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will my plan still be there if I close the browser?">
  Yes. Because your plan is saved locally in your browser, it persists between sessions on the same device and browser. You can close the tab, shut down your computer, and come back days later — your schedule will be exactly as you left it.

  <Note>
    Your plan is tied to a specific browser on a specific device. If you switch to a different browser or use a different device, you'll start with a blank weekly plan.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use FitStart Planner on my phone?">
  Absolutely. FitStart Planner uses a fully responsive design, so it adapts to any screen size — smartphone, tablet, or desktop. Just open your preferred mobile browser, navigate to the app, and everything from the Workout Library to the Weekly Planner works the same way it does on a larger screen.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reset or clear my weekly plan?">
  Navigate to the **Weekly Planner** page and tap or click the **Clear All** button. This removes every session from your Mon–Sun schedule in one step. If you only want to remove a single session, use the individual remove control next to that session instead.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What equipment do I need for the workouts?">
  Most routines in the Workout Library require no equipment at all — just your body and enough floor space to move around. The one exception is the **Starter Strength Session**, which calls for a set of light dumbbells.

  <Info>
    If you don't have dumbbells yet, you can swap the Starter Strength Session for any of the equipment-free routines while you get set up. Use the goal and level filters in the Workout Library to find a good alternative.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I can't find a workout that matches my filters — what should I do?">
  First, try broadening your filter criteria. If you have multiple filters active at once (for example, a specific goal **and** a specific level), removing one of them will expand the results. If you've also typed a keyword into the search bar, clearing it will give you the full filtered list without any text constraints.

  If you still don't see what you're looking for, browsing all six routines without any filters applied is the quickest way to get a complete picture of what's available.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the BMI calculator medically accurate?">
  The BMI calculator gives you a standard screening result based on the widely used Body Mass Index formula. Results fall into four categories: **Underweight** (below 18.5), **Normal Weight** (18.5–24.9), **Overweight** (25–29.9), and **Obese** (30 and above).

  <Warning>
    BMI is a general screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. It does not account for factors like muscle mass, bone density, age, or sex. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about your health based on BMI or any other single metric.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I sign up for the personalized workout plan newsletter?">
  Scroll to the **Start Your Journey** section on the FitStart Planner homepage. Fill in your full name, email address, and fitness goal, check the terms checkbox, then submit the form. You'll receive personalized workout plan emails tailored to the goal you selected.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What fitness goals can I choose from?">
  FitStart Planner currently supports four fitness goals across the Workout Library filters and the newsletter signup form:

  - **Weight Loss** — routines focused on calorie burn and cardiovascular effort
  - **Strength** — sessions designed to build foundational muscle
  - **Mobility** — workouts that improve flexibility and range of motion
  - **Endurance** — training that builds stamina over time

  You can filter the Workout Library by any of these goals to surface the routines most relevant to what you're working toward.
</Accordion>
