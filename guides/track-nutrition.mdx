Good nutrition doesn't have to be complicated, and FitStart Planner's nutrition page keeps it that way. You'll find three practical tools in one place: a visual guide to building a balanced plate, a hydration calculator that gives you a personalized daily water target, and a meal idea generator that takes the guesswork out of what to eat. Head to the Nutrition page to get started.

## The Balanced Plate Guide

The Balanced Plate is a simple visual framework for building a nutritious meal without counting calories or following a rigid meal plan. Aim to fill your plate like this:

| Portion | Food type |
|---|---|
| **½ of the plate** | Vegetables and fruit |
| **¼ of the plate** | Lean protein |
| **¼ of the plate** | Quality carbohydrates |
| **Small addition** | Healthy fats |

Use this as a starting point for every main meal. You don't have to be exact — the goal is a general pattern, not precision.

<Tip>
  If you're not sure what counts as a "quality carb," think minimally processed: brown rice, oats, sweet potato, whole-grain bread, or legumes all fit the bill.
</Tip>

## Hydration Calculator

Staying hydrated is one of the simplest things you can do to support your workouts and overall health. The hydration calculator estimates how many liters of water you should aim to drink each day based on your body weight and activity level.

**Formula:** `weight (kg) × 0.033 × activity multiplier = daily water target (liters)`

| Activity level | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Light | 1.0 |
| Moderate | 1.15 |
| High | 1.3 |

### Using the Calculator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your body weight">
    Type your weight in kilograms into the **Body Weight** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your activity level">
    Choose **Light**, **Moderate**, or **High** from the activity level dropdown based on how active you are on a typical day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your water target">
    The calculator displays your recommended daily water intake in liters as soon as both fields are filled in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Worked example:** A person weighing **70 kg** with a **Moderate** activity level should aim for:

> 70 × 0.033 × 1.15 ≈ **2.7 liters per day**

<Info>
  This figure covers total water intake from all sources — plain water, herbal teas, and the water content in food all contribute. On hot days or during intense sessions, lean toward the higher end of your range.
</Info>

## Meal Idea Generator

When you're not sure what to eat, the Meal Idea Generator picks a balanced, four-component meal at random from a curated set of beginner-friendly ingredients.

### Using the Generator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Generate Meal Idea">
    Press the **Generate Meal Idea** button on the nutrition page. A complete meal suggestion appears instantly, combining one item from each food group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the suggestion">
    Each generated meal includes a protein, a carbohydrate, a vegetable combination, and a healthy fat — following the balanced plate framework automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use or regenerate">
    If the suggestion suits you, use it for your next meal. If it doesn't appeal to you, click the button again for a new combination.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The generator draws from the following options:

| Component | Options |
|---|---|
| **Protein** | Grilled chicken breast, Baked salmon, Greek yogurt bowl, Tofu stir-fry |
| **Carbs** | Brown rice, Sweet potato, Quinoa, Whole-grain pasta |
| **Vegetables** | Broccoli + carrots, Mixed salad greens, Roasted peppers + onions, Spinach + cucumber |
| **Healthy Fats** | Olive oil drizzle, Avocado slices, Handful of nuts, Chia seeds |

<Tip>
  Click **Generate Meal Idea** several times in a row to build a shortlist of meals for the week. Each combination is randomly assembled, so you'll rarely get the same result twice in a row.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The meal suggestions are general inspiration, not a prescribed diet plan. If you have specific dietary requirements, allergies, or medical conditions, consult a registered dietitian before making significant changes to your eating habits.
</Note>
