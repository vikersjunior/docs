The Weekly Planner turns a loose list of workout ideas into a structured, day-by-day schedule you can actually follow. Every session you add appears on a visual board organized by day, and a running summary keeps you honest about how many sessions you've committed to and how many total minutes you're planning to move. Best of all, your plan is saved automatically — close the tab, come back tomorrow, and everything is still there.

## Opening the Weekly Planner

Navigate to the Weekly Planner page in your browser. You'll see the scheduling form on the left and the weekly board on the right (or stacked vertically on smaller screens).

## Adding a Workout Session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a day">
    In the **Day** dropdown, select the day of the week you want to schedule the session — **Monday** through **Sunday**. This field is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the workout">
    In the **Workout** text field, type the name of the routine you want to do — for example, `Beginner Cardio` or `Morning Stretch`. This field is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the duration">
    Enter the number of minutes in the **Duration** field. The accepted range is **5 to 180 minutes**. This field is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add optional notes">
    Use the **Notes** textarea to jot down anything personal — a focus cue, a modification, or a reminder. This field is optional and can be left blank.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the session">
    Click the **Add Session** button. The session card immediately appears on the weekly board under the day you selected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reading the Weekly Board

Each session card on the board displays:

- **Workout name** — the text you entered in the Workout field
- **Duration** — shown in minutes
- **Notes** — displayed beneath the workout name if you added any
- **Remove button** — deletes that individual session from the board

Below the board, the **summary line** gives you a quick snapshot of your week, for example:

> _3 sessions planned • 55 total minutes_

The session count and total minutes update automatically every time you add or remove a session.

<Info>
  The summary line counts every session across all days, not just the ones visible on screen. Use it as a quick gut-check before committing to your week.
</Info>

## Removing Sessions

**To remove a single session**, click the **Remove** button on that session's card. The card disappears immediately and the summary line recalculates.

**To wipe the entire plan**, click the **Clear All** button. This removes every session from every day at once.

<Warning>
  Clicking **Clear All** is permanent — there is no undo. Double-check your plan before clearing it if you want to keep any sessions.
</Warning>

## How Your Plan Is Saved

FitStart Planner saves your weekly schedule automatically to your browser's local storage. You don't need an account, and there's nothing to manually export or sync.

<Tip>
  Because the data lives in your browser, your plan persists when you close the tab and reopen it later — even after restarting your computer. Just return to the Weekly Planner page in the same browser on the same device and your sessions will be right where you left them.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Your saved plan is specific to the browser and device you're using. If you switch to a different browser or a different device, you'll start with a blank plan. Clearing your browser's site data will also erase your saved schedule.
</Note>
