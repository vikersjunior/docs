The FitStart Planner [homepage](/) is more than a welcome screen — it puts three handy fitness tools right in front of you the moment you arrive. Use the BMI Calculator to understand where you're starting from, the Random Workout Generator to remove decision fatigue, and the Daily Fitness Tip Generator to pick up a useful habit reminder whenever you need one. No sign-in or setup required for any of them.

## BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple screening number calculated from your height and weight. It's a quick way to see how your current weight relates to a healthy range for your height. FitStart Planner calculates it for you instantly.

### How to Use It

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your height">
    Type your height in **centimetres** into the Height field. For example, someone who is 5 ft 8 in would enter `173`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your weight">
    Type your weight in **kilograms** into the Weight field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calculate">
    Click **Calculate BMI**. Your BMI value and its corresponding category appear immediately below the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### BMI Categories

| BMI Range | Category |
|---|---|
| Below 18.5 | Underweight |
| 18.5 – 24.9 | Normal Weight |
| 25.0 – 29.9 | Overweight |
| 30.0 and above | Obese |

<Warning>
  BMI is a population-level screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. It does not account for muscle mass, bone density, age, sex, or body composition. A very muscular person may show a high BMI while being perfectly healthy. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health decisions based on your BMI result.
</Warning>

<Info>
  The standard BMI formula is: **BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)²**. For example, a person who is 170 cm tall and weighs 68 kg has a BMI of 68 ÷ (1.70)² = 23.5, which falls in the Normal Weight category.
</Info>

## Random Workout Generator {#random-workout-generator}

Choosing a workout is sometimes the hardest part of exercising. The Random Workout Generator removes that friction entirely — one click gives you a randomly selected routine from the Workout Library.

### How to Use It

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Generate Workout">
    Find the **Random Workout Generator** section on the homepage and click the **Generate Workout** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your suggestion">
    The tool displays a randomly chosen beginner routine, including its name, duration, goal, and level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on it">
    Head straight to the [Workout Library](/features/workout-library) to see the full exercise list for that routine, or open the [Weekly Planner](/features/weekly-planner) to schedule it for today.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the first suggestion doesn't appeal to you, click **Generate Workout** again. Each click picks independently from all six routines in the library, so you might get a different one straight away.
</Tip>

## Daily Fitness Tip Generator

Building lasting fitness habits is as much about mindset as it is about exercise. The Daily Fitness Tip Generator surfaces one practical tip each time you click the button — a quick nudge to keep you motivated and on track.

### How to Use It

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Get a Tip">
    Find the **Daily Fitness Tip** section on the homepage and click **Get a Tip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your tip">
    A tip drawn from a curated set of 12 beginner-friendly reminders appears on screen. Examples include:
    - *"Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily."*
    - *"Start small — 10 minutes of exercise is better than none."*
    - *"Consistency beats intensity."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply it today">
    Choose one tip to focus on for the rest of the day. Small, consistent actions add up to big results over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each click selects a tip randomly from the full set of 12. You may occasionally see the same tip twice in a row — that's a good sign it's worth remembering.
</Note>

The three homepage tools work best when you treat them as a quick-start routine before your workout:
