The Workout Library is your starting point for every session in FitStart Planner. You'll find six carefully designed routines that cover the full range of beginner fitness needs — from gentle morning stretches to introductory strength training. Each routine lists its duration, required equipment, and every individual exercise so you always know exactly what to expect before you begin.

## Finding the Right Workout

Use the three filter controls at the top of the [Workout Library page](/features/workout-library) to narrow down the list instantly. You can combine all three filters at the same time.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Filter by Goal" icon="bullseye">
    Choose from **All Goals**, **Weight Loss**, **Strength**, **Mobility**, or **Endurance** to match a routine to your current objective.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter by Level" icon="signal">
    Select **All Levels**, **Beginner**, **Easy**, or **Intermediate** to make sure the session suits where you are right now.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keyword Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Type any word into the search box to match against a routine's name, description, or individual exercise names.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Not sure where to start? Leave all filters on their default "All" settings and use the [Random Workout Generator](/features/fitness-tools#random-workout-generator) on the homepage to get an instant suggestion.
</Tip>

## The Six Routines

Every routine in the library is designed with beginners in mind. The table below gives you a quick overview of all six sessions.

| Routine | Duration | Goal | Level | Equipment |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Morning Stretch Routine | 10 min | Mobility | Beginner | None |
| Beginner Cardio Blast | 20 min | Endurance | Easy | Bodyweight |
| Core Strength Builder | 15 min | Strength | Beginner | Mat |
| Fat Burn Circuit | 25 min | Weight Loss | Intermediate | None |
| Walking Recovery Flow | 18 min | Mobility | Easy | None |
| Starter Strength Session | 22 min | Strength | Beginner | Light dumbbells |

### Routine Details

<Accordion title="Morning Stretch Routine — 10 min · Mobility · Beginner">
  A gentle, equipment-free sequence that wakes up your joints and improves flexibility. Perfect as a daily warm-up or a standalone session on rest days.

  **Exercises:**
  1. Neck circles
  2. Cat-cow
  3. Hip opener stretch
  4. Child's pose
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Beginner Cardio Blast — 20 min · Endurance · Easy">
  A low-impact cardio routine that gets your heart rate up using only your bodyweight. No jumping required for most moves, making it joint-friendly for true beginners.

  **Exercises:**
  1. March in place
  2. Step jacks
  3. Mountain climbers
  4. Bodyweight squats
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Core Strength Builder — 15 min · Strength · Beginner">
  Targets your deep core muscles with slow, controlled movements. A mat is the only equipment you need. These exercises build the stable foundation that every other workout depends on.

  **Exercises:**
  1. Dead bug
  2. Bird-dog
  3. Forearm plank
  4. Glute bridge
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Fat Burn Circuit — 25 min · Weight Loss · Intermediate">
  A higher-intensity circuit designed to elevate your heart rate and burn calories. This is the most demanding routine in the library — complete the easier routines first before attempting this one.

  **Exercises:**
  1. Jumping jacks
  2. Alternating lunges
  3. Pushups
  4. High knees

  <Warning>
    This is the only **Intermediate** routine in the library. If you are brand new to exercise, build a few weeks of consistency with the Beginner and Easy routines before adding this session to your plan.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Walking Recovery Flow — 18 min · Mobility · Easy">
  Combines light walking with targeted stretches to help your muscles recover between harder sessions. Equipment-free and suitable for any day of the week.

  **Exercises:**
  1. Brisk walk
  2. Calf stretch
  3. Quad stretch
  4. Hamstring reach
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Starter Strength Session — 22 min · Strength · Beginner">
  Introduces fundamental dumbbell movements at a beginner-friendly pace. A pair of light dumbbells is all the equipment you need to complete this full-body session.

  **Exercises:**
  1. Goblet squat
  2. Dumbbell row
  3. Shoulder press
  4. Romanian deadlift

  <Note>
    Choose a weight that lets you complete all repetitions with good form but still feels challenging by the last rep. When in doubt, go lighter.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

## Adding a Routine to Your Plan

Once you've found a routine you like, head over to the [Weekly Planner](/features/weekly-planner) to schedule it on a specific day. You can add the same routine on multiple days and set a custom duration and notes for each session.
