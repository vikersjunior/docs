## Start in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open FitStart Planner">
    Visit the FitStart Planner homepage in your browser. No sign-up or download required — it works instantly on desktop and mobile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Tools">
    Head to the **Workouts** page to find a routine that fits your goal, or use the homepage tools to calculate your BMI and get a daily tip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Your Week">
    Go to the **Weekly Plan** page, add your chosen workout sessions to each day, and save your schedule. Your plan is saved automatically in your browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  FitStart Planner saves your weekly schedule locally in your browser. Your plan persists between visits — no account needed.
</Tip>
